2WD RC Car — First Model

Personal electromechanical project focused on design, control systems, and testing.

Overview

This project involved the design and construction of a 2-wheel-drive RC car capable of reaching approximately 20 km/h. The system integrates mechanical design, electronics, and control logic.

Control System

  • FlySky transmitter and receiver
  • DC motor with electronic speed controller
  • Servo-based steering
  • PWM-based speed control

Tools & Skills

  • SolidWorks CAD
  • 3D Printing
  • Electronics integration
  • Testing and iteration
