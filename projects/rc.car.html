2WD RC Car — First Iteration

Personal electromechanical project focused on design, control, and testing.

Overview

This project involved the design and construction of a 2-wheel-drive RC car capable of reaching approximately 20 km/h. The system integrates mechanical design, electrical hardware, and control logic.

Control System

  • FlySky transmitter and receiver
  • Microcontroller-based motor control
  • PWM speed control and steering servo

Tools & Skills

  • SolidWorks CAD
  • 3D printing
  • Electronics integration
  • Testing and iteration
